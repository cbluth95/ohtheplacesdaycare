About our Toddler Program
(24 - 36 months)

Here at Oh, the Places you will go our Toddler Program is focused on getting kids to use their words, sharing, and playing well with others. We do encourage all age groups to make good choices. We use buzz words throughout the facility starting with the infants. We do try go give the children in this age group more choices in their day. We give them options and all of those options are options that the teachers approve. We do give this age group a little more wiggle room to make their own choices.

This age group focuses more on recognizing colors, working on numbers, and learning the ABCâ€™s as much as possible. This age group does still take naps when they will actually sleep. We do not force children to nap so quiet time activities are provided to those that will not sleep. If kiddo is still in diapers we do diaper changes three times a day for this age group. We also change diapers as needed.

With potty training being more prevalent in this age group we do ask any kiddos training if they need to potty at least hourly. We do ask more frequently for those that are just starting to train. We do work with this age group on cleaning up the room, sitting better at the table, and throwing away their own trash at snack and lunch.

This age group works a lot with improving the words that the kiddos speak as well as learning newer words that can be a little more difficult. Please contact Kristin our director with any further questions.
