About our Wobbler Program
(12 - 24 months)

Here at Oh, the Places you will go our Wobbler Program is focused on fine motor skills, large motor skills, and getting kiddos where they need to be developmentally. We start thinking spots with this age group to help teach boundaries. This age group works more on learning words, walking if not already walking once out of the infant room, sharing toys, and just exploring the world around them.

Free play is a good tool used in this as well as some age appropriate curriculum written by the providers in the classrooms. All curriculum is approved through the director on a weekly basis. We let the Wobblers work on threading, they work with pipe cleaners and colanders, and also with blocks to work on stacking. Wobblers start to transition to tables from high chairs. We work with this age group on staying at the table as well.

All meals are served home style with the teachers actually at the tables giving more encouragement for the children to actually stay at the tables. Diaper changes are done three times a day with this age group and also when needed. We do ask that parents lead when to start potty training and it can be started with this age group. All potty trainers must be in regular underwear. They do wear pull ups but only at nap time. Please contact Kristin our director with any further questions.
